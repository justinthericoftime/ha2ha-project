Core Principles

Five principles guide every HA2HA design decision.

1

Humans First

Every cross-boundary action requires human approval

2

Trust is Earned

Unknown agents start blocked; trust builds over time

3

Fail Secure

When in doubt, block and escalate to humans

4

Audit Everything

Complete trails for forensics and accountability

5

Mutual Verification

Both ends must have human oversight

Trust Levels

Trust is earned through demonstrated good behavior, never assumed.

Level Name Cooldown Description
0 Blocked Permanent No communication allowed
1 Unknown 24 hours Every request needs explicit approval
2 Provisional 4 hours Approval required, enhanced monitoring
3 Standard 1 hour Normal operation with approval
4 Trusted 15 min Expedited approval, pre-approved categories
5 Verified 5 min Maximum trust, still requires approval
